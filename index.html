About USDT.z

USDT.z (Tether USD Bridged ZED20) provides a stable, low-fee, and fast token for trading and on-chain transfers on Trust Wallet.

Key Features

Stability

Backed by a bridged stable model, USDT.z offers price reliability and trust.

Low Fees

Supports fast, low-fee transactions on Trust Wallet and other BEP20 wallets.

Community Driven

Governed and promoted by the community for long-term sustainability.
